MAD BURGER

MAD BURGER IS AN AMERICAN STYLE BURGER JOINT THAT IN A VERY SHORT TIME HAS MADE ITS WAY AMONG THE BEST BURGER STORES IN LIMA, PERU.
THE BRAND OPENED ITS FIRST STORE OVERSEAS; IN WYNWOOD, MIAMI (USA).

THE BRAND WAS CREATED ON MAY 28, 2020 BY JAIME PESAQUE, THE RENOWNED CHEF AND OWNER OF «MAYTA» (RESTAURANT BELONGING TO THE PRESTIGIOUS LIST «THE WORLD’S 50 BEST RESTAURANTS» SINCE 2022).

ON THE MENU YOU WILL FIND HANDCRAFTED BURGERS WITH THE AUTHENTIC FLAVOR OF THE CLASSIC AMERICAN BURGER, WHICH CAN BE ACCOMPANIED WITH FRIES, SHAKES AND MORE.

MAD BURGER CONTINUES ITS EXPANSION PLAN AND HAS 4 LOCATIONS IN PERÚ AND 1 LOCATION IN USA.
